Why is
BruhProx not working

Welcome to Support Article KB34128

Hey There! Welcome to Article KB34128. This article is about Why BruhProx for Replit, Glitch, and Cloudflare Pages is unavalible

What Happened

Introduction

Basicly it all originated back in September 2022 When Replit one of our major host providers started cracking down on Proxys since they use alot of bandwidth. And then two weeks into September they made it against the TOS to have a web proxy which caused BruhProx to stop working and eventualy to be Shut Down in November 2022. With the Release of BruhProx 6 we Deployed it to glitch since at the time Glitch was a very small (and still is) community! Everything was going well including the release of BruhProx Core in March 2023. However all good things must come to an end. On April 19th 2023 All Instances of Web Proxys were taken down by Glitch for violating the new TOS. Which of course leads us to today.

Whats Next?

At the time of writing this I've tried to deploy BruhProx to Cyclic, Koyeb, and CloudFlare Pages with none being successfull since Cyclic was being finicy about the Package.json file, Koyeb required a Credit Card for "Trial Purposes" which of course I want to keep making z1g Services Free but that also means we need to use Free Hosts. and of Course CloudFlare Pages (Also known as pages.dev) and According to a similar project that BruhProx was based off of UltraViolet, They state that Vercel and Cloudflare Pages aren't supported because they aren't static Websites and are Powered by NodeJS. Because of that we were unable to deploy bruhprox and it just displays the server offline page on it.

Will BruhProx Return?

Once Freenom is done getting sued and the services start working again Ill try and get an actual domain probably bruhprox.z1g-project.tk or something along those lines! If you want to stay up to date with the latest z1g Project Updates visit our discord: Here
