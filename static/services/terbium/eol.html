Terbium
Version End of Life

What Happened

Introduction

Basicly after all of the recent complaints for bug fixes and patches for Legacy Terbium Replit which I've been trying to deprecate for weeks now and the whole entire repl/github is so messy at that point its being held together by nothing and at this point I'm just tired to having to go back and maintain something that barely functions anymore to the point where I'm fed up.

Why?

As of May 15th 2023 We are deprecating all support for Legacy Terbium Builds such as Legacy Terbium for Replit, v1.42, v1.43, v1.44 in favor of our much faster and matured version: Terbium v1.45 so if you want to update feel free to check out Migration Options in the Update App or Below with all the links for Self Hosting and Forking Options. We chose to Deprecate these because of the fact that Terbium has matured and is up to 57% Faster than v1.44 and the Preview and Beta versions of v1.45 which are still supported btw are 20-48% Faster than v1.44 depending on which version you chose. We understand that for some people its hard to migrate but we are recommending it in a 1 Week advance. After May 15th 2023 Any issues, bugs or feature requests for Legacy Terbium Builds won't be looked at and simply rejected.
